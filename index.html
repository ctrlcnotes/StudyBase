About StudyBase

StudyBase is a free, no-login student hub built for Filipino high school and senior high school students. No ads, no fluff -- just tools and a space to connect.

What's inside:

  • --Pomodoro Timer -- study in 25-minute focused blocks with built-in break modes.
  • --Quick Notes -- a scratch pad for ideas, reminders, or anything you need to jot down fast.
  • --Study Tips -- short, research-backed tips that actually work in practice.
  • --Discussion Board -- leave comments, ask questions, or share tips with other students.

This site was made as a simple, honest resource for students. It is not perfect, but it gets the job done. If something is not working or you have a suggestion, drop it in the comments.

Tips

Study Smarter, Not Harder

01.

Active recall beats re-reading

After studying, close your notes and try to recall everything from memory. It feels harder -- that's exactly why it works.

02.

Space out your reviews

Review material after 1 day, 3 days, then a week. Spaced repetition is one of the most research-backed study methods.

03.

Phone in another room -- actually

Even a face-down phone on the desk drains your focus. Put it away. The notifs will still be there in 25 minutes.

04.

Sleep consolidates memory

Your brain processes what you learned while you sleep. Studying until 2am almost always backfires. Sleep, then review.

05.

Teach it out loud

Try explaining a concept to yourself as if you're teaching it. If you can't explain it simply, you don't fully understand it yet.

06.

Chunk big tasks

"Study for the exam" is too vague. Try "review Chapter 3 definitions" -- specific tasks are easier to start and finish.
